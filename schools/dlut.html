学校简介

University Overview

Dalian University of Technology (DUT) is a prestigious research-oriented comprehensive university directly under China's Ministry of Education. It is a Project 985/211 and national "Double First-Class" university. Three disciplines — Mechanics, Mechanical Engineering, and Chemical Engineering — are national Double First-Class construction disciplines. Engineering has entered ESI global top 0.01%, with 15 disciplines in ESI Top 1%. DUT offers two pathways: Chinese-taught (41 majors) and English-taught (10 majors).
